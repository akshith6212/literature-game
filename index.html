      How to Play

      Ask: Pick an opponent + card you don't have (from a half-suit you hold).

      Hit: They give you the card, keep your turn.

      Miss: Turn passes to them.

      Declare: Assign all 6 cards of a half-suit to teammates. Correct = your team scores!
